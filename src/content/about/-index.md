Welcome to FGB (FistingGuide Blog) — a purely online blog platform dedicated to Gay Fisting.This is a specialized blog created for Gay Fisting enthusiasts, focused on knowledge sharing and experience exchange. We publish articles in the form of real personal stories, practical guides, safety information, and community support content.

On FGB , you can read:
- Real Personal Stories: Anonymous contributions from community members sharing their firsthand experiences — from the nervousness and excitement of beginners to the insights and reflections of experienced players.
- Beginner’s Guide: Comprehensive tutorials covering everything from basic knowledge to advanced techniques, including relaxation methods, lubricant selection, position suggestions, toy recommendations, and detailed illustrated guides.
- Risks and Safety Knowledge: Honest and thorough explanations of potential health risks, prevention measures, and correct response methods to help everyone enjoy the experience while protecting themselves to the greatest extent possible.
- Community Support Articles: Practical content on partner communication skills, psychological adjustment, frequently asked questions, and more.
- Submissions and Co-creation: We warmly welcome submissions from all readers! Whether it’s personal stories, experience summaries, tutorial insights, or questions and answers, feel free to contact us to submit. All submissions will be reviewed before being published as blog articles, and we strictly protect the privacy of all contributors.

FGB’s Core Values is Safety First, Informed Consent, Mutual Respect, and Non-Discrimination.
